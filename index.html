"Bold" and "Italics" tags were replaced by "Strong" and "Emphasis" tags in HTML5 because the Bold tag and the italic tag are listed as a WCAG compliance Level A error. This is because most screen readers will NOT announce these changes to the screen reader user. The Strong tag, and the Emphasis tag, are considered Semantic Markup that allows for added meaning to content. It serves as an indication to a screen reader of how something should be understood.
